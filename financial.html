Step by Step

How to Get Work in Ethiopia

1

Create Your ECA Profile

Register as a cadet member. Add your rank, sea service history, vessel types, and certificates. Takes 5 minutes.

2

Upload Your Maritime CV

Upload your updated CV and key documents — COC, STCW certificates, seaman's book, discharge book summary.

3

Set Your Availability Window

Tell employers exactly when you are home in Ethiopia and when you need to return to sea. Be specific — it gets you matched faster.

4

Browse & Apply for Jobs

Filter roles by category, duration, and location. Apply directly through ECA — no agent fees, no middlemen.

5

Work, Earn & Return to Sea

Complete your contract, add it to your Career Tracker, then return to your ship career — stronger and with extra income.
